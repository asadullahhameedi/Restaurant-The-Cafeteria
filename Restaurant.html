Our Story

A Legacy of Flavor

Founded in 2024, The Cafeteria began as a dream to blend traditional Afghan hospitality with modern culinary innovation. Our kitchen is led by Executive Chef Asadullah Hameedi, whose passion for clean, efficient code translates into precise, artful cuisine.

We source locally, cook with passion, and serve with grace. Every ingredient tells a story, every dish is a conversation, and every visit leaves a lasting impression.
